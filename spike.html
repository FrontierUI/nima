proudly serving as our Licensed Service Animal and beloved mascot.

Spike's heartwarming journey started when he was lovingly Adopted by our Aesthetician, Mindi, when he was just 5 weeks old.

From that moment, a lifelong bond was formed, and Spike quickly became an essential part of our family.

Spike's presence at NIMA is more than just a delightful wag of the tail; he embodies the very essence of our mission. As a Licensed Service Animal, Spike brings an extra layer of comfort, support, and positivity to our space. His unwavering companionship serves as a reminder of the empathy and compassion we extend to every client who walks through our doors.

Beyond being a furry friend, Spike is a true inspiration. His gentle nature and ability to connect with people, especially those who need extra care, highlight the core values of Newport Integrative Medicine & Aesthetics.. His tail seems to wag with excitement as he witnesses the transformational journeys of our clients, mirroring the joy we all feel when witnessing newfound confidence and beauty.
